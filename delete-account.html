Delete Your EnQMail Account

Permanently remove your account and all associated data.

Deletion is irreversible

Delete Account From Within The App

EnQMail provides a built-in account deletion feature.

To delete your account:

  1. Open EnQMail
  2. Go to Settings & Rules
  3. Open Privacy & Safety
  4. Select Delete My Account & Data
  5. Confirm the deletion request

Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

What Data Is Deleted?

When an account deletion request is completed, EnQMail permanently deletes:

  • User account information
  • Connected Gmail account records
  • Stored email records
  • Email body content
  • Folder assignments
  • Custom folders
  • Review Queue data
  • Needs Action records
  • Save For Reference records
  • Reminder configurations
  • Routing rules
  • Blocked sender rules
  • User preferences and settings

Deletion Timeline

Account deletion occurs immediately through EnQMail's backend systems. After deletion, the data cannot be recovered.
